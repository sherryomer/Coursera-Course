Our menu

Chicken

Indulge in our succulent chicken dishes that cater to all tastes. Enjoy the Grilled Chicken Breast, tender and juicy, accompanied by a refreshing garden salad. For a crunchier delight, savor the Crispy Fried Chicken, served with your choice of dipping sauce. Delve into the richness of our Chicken Alfredo Pasta, where creamy Alfredo sauce coats perfectly grilled chicken and fettuccine pasta. Or, opt for the BBQ Chicken Wings, irresistibly flavored with our signature BBQ sauce, complemented by crisp celery sticks and a side of tangy blue cheese dressing

Beef

Savor the flavors of our mouthwatering beef offerings. Relish the Steakhouse Burger, a succulent beef patty crowned with cheddar cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and a special sauce, served with golden fries. Experience the delightful blend of tastes in our Beef Stir-Fry, tender beef slices combined with a medley of vibrant vegetables in a delectable stir-fry sauce, accompanied by steamed rice. For a delightful Mexican twist, try our Beef Tacos, featuring seasoned ground beef nestled in soft corn tortillas, garnished with fresh lettuce, cheese, and a zesty salsa. Experience the unique taste of our Beef Kebabs, skewers of marinated beef, bell peppers, and onions, grilled to perfection, and served with a delightful side of rice pilaf.

Sushi

Delight your palate with our exquisite sushi creations. The California Roll, a harmonious blend of avocado, cucumber, and imitation crab meat, wrapped in seaweed and sushi rice, topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds. Experience a delightful fusion of flavors in our Spicy Tuna Roll, featuring fresh tuna infused with spicy mayo, alongside cool cucumber and creamy avocado, all rolled in seasoned sushi rice and nori. Savor the simplicity of our Salmon Nigiri, offering fresh slices of premium salmon atop a bed of perfectly seasoned sushi rice. For a divine treat, explore the Dragon Roll, boasting eel, avocado, and cucumber on the inside, topped with luscious slices of avocado and drizzled with a delectable eel sauce.
